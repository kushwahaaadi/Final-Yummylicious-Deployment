Mission

Why We Exist

Yummylicious was created to solve a very real problem on campus — the time lost standing in food court queues. Every minute you spend waiting is a minute you could spend studying, resting, or doing something you actually enjoy.

Browse any campus food court, pick your time slot, place your order, and walk in only when it's ready. Simple as that.

How It Works

About the Creator

Made at Bennett University

This project was built by a student who wanted a better experience at the campus food courts. It started as a simple idea and grew into a full ordering platform covering all six food courts on campus — with order tracking, time slots, and a token wallet.

If you have feedback, ideas, or want to collaborate — reach out. This is a living project and it gets better with every suggestion.
