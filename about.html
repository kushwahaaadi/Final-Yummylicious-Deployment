Mission

Why We Exist

Yummylicious was created to solve a very real problem on campus — the time lost standing in food court queues. Every minute you spend waiting is a minute you could spend studying, resting, or doing something you actually enjoy.

Browse any campus food court, place your order in advance, and walk in only when it's ready. Simple as that.
